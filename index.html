I am currently affiliated with the Institute of Science and Technology for Brain-Inspired Intelligence (ISTBI) and the School of Data Science, Fudan University, serving as the Director of Professional Training Program.

I earned my Ph.D. in Statistics from Texas A&M University, under the supervision of Prof. Jianhua Huang. I then held a postdoctoral research position for one year before joining Fudan. Prior to this, I obtained a B.S. in Mathematics and a B.A. in Economics from Peking University.

My research lies at the intersection of statistics and artificial intelligence, with a focus on nonparametric methods, statistical machine learning, and functional data analysis. A recent area of strong interest involves modeling noisy data with physical mechanisms governed by differential equations, particularly for its applications in Digital Twin Brain.

I am actively seeking motivated students to join my research group. If you are interested, please send me an email with your CV.
